Summary

When we say cookies, this doesn't mean actual eating cookies, but cookies that are small text files stored on your device by websites. They help with authentication, personalization, and tracking.

Key Terms

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Stored longer to remember preferences.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Used for tracking across websites.
